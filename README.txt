Product Name: BookPortal

Description:
BookPortal is a website and mobile application that allows users to find any book of any category and genre through its virtual library. Users can search for books in many ways, and they can have access to them even if they're offline by downloading them on the mobile application on any device. Subscription plans are made affordable to ensure that reading is accessible to everyone.
